# XD.AI Brand Styles Reference

## Typography

### Fonts
- **Body text**: Causten, 11pt
- **Heading 1**: Causten Bold, 20pt, color #3313E2 (brand blue)
- **Heading 2**: Causten Medium, 16pt, black
- **Heading 3**: Causten Medium, 14pt, black
- **Code**: Consolas, 10pt

### Spacing
- **Normal paragraphs**: Line spacing 240 twips (single)
- **Heading 1**: 360 twips before, 80 after
- **Heading 2-3**: 160 twips before, 80 after
- **List items**: 120 twips before and after
